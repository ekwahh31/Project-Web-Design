TOP BRANDS

Lamborghini

Founded in 1963, Automobili Lamborghini is headquartered in Sant’Agata Bolognese, in Northern Italy. Lamborghini currently produces two super sports car models, the V12-powered Aventador, launched in 2011, and the V10-powered Huracán, launched in 2014, along with the Urus Super SUV powered by a twin-turbo V8 engine, launched in 2017.

Ferrari

Founded in 1947, Ferrari is headquartered in Maranello, Italy, in the Emilia-Romagna region. Ferrari currently produces two super sports car models, the V12-powered SF90 Stradale, launched in 2019, and the V8-powered F8 Tributo, launched in 2019 as well, along with the Purosangue SUV powered by a twin-turbo V8 engine, set to launch in 2022.

Porsche

Founded in 1931, Porsche is headquartered in Stuttgart, Germany. Porsche currently produces two super sports car models, the flat-six-powered 911 Turbo S, launched in 2020, and the V8-powered 718 Cayman GT4, launched in 2019, along with the Cayenne Turbo SUV powered by a twin-turbo V8 engine, launched in 2018.
